Page VIII

Recommendations & action plan

What the data recommends we do next — organized by what is working, what needs optimization, and where to invest budget.

What is working

Demand capture & high-intent search

  • Google PMAX campaign is the workhorse — 114 conversions on €3,031, driving the Early Booking Offer cleanly.
  • Meta "Website Traffic · Worldwide" set delivers LP views at €0.08 apiece — best efficiency across the account.
  • UK, Netherlands, US and Ireland produce highest revenue with minimal paid dependence — organic/direct strength.
  • Desktop revenue is €227K vs €60K mobile despite half the sessions — guests booking from desktop convert materially better.
What needs optimization

Tracking & attribution gaps

  • Google Ads conversion value reads €1.02 per conversion — booking engine isn't passing purchase value back. Fix server-side or Enhanced Conversions.
  • GA4 shows 0 purchases attributed to Paid Search despite 1,681 paid sessions — attribution resets at booking engine redirect. Configure cross-domain or upgrade to GA4 referral exclusion.
  • "Unassigned" channel holds €78K / 38 bookings — requires UTM audit and default-channel rule review.
  • Mobile booking engine step 2 drops 68% of sessions — highest single revenue leak.
Recommended budget shifts

Where to move investment

  • Increase Google PMAX daily budget by ~30% while cost per booking holds under €80.
  • Hold Meta budget — continue optimizing for LP views and assisted contribution, not direct ROAS.
  • Re-allocate 20% of Meta Page Growth spend into offer-specific Website Traffic sets (Summer, Spa, Beach Club).
  • Pause post-boost activity below €15 individual spend — no measurable commercial lift.
Scale these opportunities

Offers, markets, creative

  • Scale: Early Booking Offer, Summer Escape, Spa Wellness — all above 22x ROAS.
  • Markets to scale: Netherlands, Ireland, UK, US, Switzerland — strong conversion, low cost per booking.
  • Creative: break Worldwide set into per-offer creative variants. Produce one premium video per offer for Q3.
  • Funnel: prioritize mobile booking engine UX audit — every 10pt reduction in step-2 drop ≈ €21K monthly revenue lift at current traffic.
“

The fastest path to measurable commercial lift is fixing conversion tracking first, then scaling PMAX with confidence — the current data says PMAX is already the strongest revenue engine, and attribution fixes will unlock the real ROAS picture needed to justify larger budgets.
