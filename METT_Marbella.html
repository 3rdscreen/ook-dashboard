Page VIII

Recommendations & action plan

What the data recommends we do next — what is working, what to optimize, and where to invest.

What is working

Paid media is driving the business

  • Paid media drives 82.5% of revenue — 116 of 141 bookings from Meta or Google Ads.
  • Google PMAX leads volume — 42 bookings and €87,620 revenue on €3,031 spend.
  • Meta generates demand efficiently — 47 attributed bookings at €0.12 CPC.
  • UK, Netherlands, US, and Ireland are the top revenue markets with minimal wasted spend.
What needs optimization

Tracking & funnel gaps

  • Google Ads conversion value reads €1.02 per conversion — booking engine isn't passing purchase value back.
  • GA4 default attribution classifies chain-domain revenue as referral — add cross-domain tracking.
  • Synxis booking engine on a separate subdomain — 7,381 sessions but zero attributable purchases. Configure GA4 cross-domain tracking and add referral exclusion for reservations.sunsethotelsandresorts.com.
  • Malformed booking URL receives 1,010 sessions per month (likely a broken redirect/canonical) — fix the source link.
  • (not set) landing page bucket: 971 sessions at 96% bounce — investigate tracker spam or misfired GTM tag.
  • Unassigned revenue needs UTM audit.
Budget shifts

Where to move investment

  • Increase Google PMAX budget by ~30% — cost per booking holds under €80.
  • Hold Meta budget, optimize toward high assisted contribution.
  • Re-allocate 20% of Page Growth spend into offer-specific Website Traffic sets.
  • Pause post boosts under €15 spend — no measurable commercial lift.
Scale opportunities

Offers, markets, creative

  • Offers: Early Booking, Summer Escape, Spa Wellness — all above 22x ROAS.
  • Markets: Netherlands, Ireland, UK, US, Switzerland — strong conversion, low cost.
  • Creative: break Worldwide set into per-offer variants. One premium video per offer.
  • Funnel: fix cross-domain tracking first — without it, every other booking-engine optimization is invisible to GA4.
“

The fastest path to measurable commercial lift is fixing conversion tracking, then scaling PMAX and top Meta ad sets with confidence. Paid media already drives over 80% of revenue.
