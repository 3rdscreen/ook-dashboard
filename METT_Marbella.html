Page VIII

Recommendations & action plan

What the data recommends we do next — what is working, what to optimize, and where to invest.

What is working

Paid media is driving the business

  • Paid media drives 82.5% of revenue — 116 of 141 bookings originated from Meta or Google Ads.
  • Google PMAX leads volume — 42 bookings and €87,620 revenue on €3,031 spend (28.9x ROAS).
  • Meta generates demand efficiently — 47 attributed bookings at €0.12 CPC, strongest ad-set CTR on USA/UK targeting.
  • UK, Netherlands, US, and Ireland are the top revenue markets with minimal wasted spend.
What needs optimization

Tracking & funnel gaps

  • Google Ads conversion value reads €1.02 per conversion — booking engine isn't passing purchase value back. Fix via Enhanced Conversions or server-side GTM.
  • GA4 default attribution classifies chain-domain revenue as referral — add cross-domain tracking or referral exclusion for the booking engine.
  • Mobile booking engine step 2 drops 68% of sessions — single biggest funnel leak.
  • "Unassigned" revenue needs UTM audit and default channel rule review.
Recommended budget shifts

Where to move investment

  • Increase Google PMAX budget by ~30% — cost per booking holds comfortably under €80.
  • Hold Meta budget, optimize toward ad sets with highest assisted contribution.
  • Re-allocate 20% of Page Growth spend into offer-specific Website Traffic sets.
  • Pause post boosts under €15 individual spend — no measurable commercial lift.
Scale these opportunities

Offers, markets, creative

  • Scale offers: Early Booking, Summer Escape, Spa Wellness — all above 22x ROAS.
  • Scale markets: Netherlands, Ireland, UK, US, Switzerland — strong conversion, low cost.
  • Creative: break Worldwide set into per-offer creative variants. Produce one premium video per offer for Q3.
  • Funnel: every 10pt reduction in mobile step-2 drop ≈ €21,000 monthly revenue lift.
“

The fastest path to measurable commercial lift is fixing conversion tracking, then scaling PMAX and top Meta ad sets with confidence. Paid media already drives over 80% of revenue — attribution fixes will unlock the real ROAS picture needed to justify larger budgets.
